Optimization

  • HP provides majority of your point total. This means you need to have HP gear on all your main team to get a high score
  • After, there's a few minor optimization to ensure you can patience ALL AOE Attacks. This will be key to scoring 2k+
  • Every point of HP helps and the defensive costumes will help in hitting the threshold to patience all attacks. The defensive costumes (Headpiece and Outfit) are helpful but not necessary. Grab them only if you have diamonds to spare
  • 2m Box CP
  • You can use a R gear to mitigate the boss damage since Resistance gives more effective mitigation
  • A SSR gear necklace with high defense subs also work but for the Earring slot a R gear earring with full resist will outdo a SSR gear with full defense
  • HP food

Normal & Hard

6/6 Valenti

  • This comp will have consistently higher HP totals than mono green for 2 main reasons:
    • Valenty is a human and Howzer gives basic stats up to all 4 units
    • With Valenty and a bit of resistance gear, even the mono Red comp can patience all attacks on hard. This will up your score from 2.1k to 2.2k or even possibly 2.3k with UR stamps (WHALY).
  • This is the difference for MAX pp flex Gboss. Howzer and Valenty both have UR stamps available so this is how some guilds are scoring above and beyond
  • THIS IS NOT RECOMMENDED. The stamps are niche choices because HP/Def Howzer is only good for CC bloating. Valetny with HP/Def is also another set of points that you can gain

4+/6 rGilthunder

  • Pre-Setup: RNG FISH, Make sure the boss only does 3 Single Target cards on turn 1
  • This way, corrossion won't affect your units because you'll kill turn 3 and Gil will tank all 3 Single target attacks. Use HP food, res food is useless
  • This only works if your Gil can 1 shot the boss otherwise, the other comps will be a better alternative. This will however score the higher with SSR gear and even higher with UR gear
  • T3 kill like normal, buff with arthur and yeet

Extreme

Phase 1

  • Valenty needs to be 6/6, Escanor can be 5/6
  • T1: Arthur Buff Escanor, Escanor Breaks Shield, Valenty Debuff and Jillian Pierce
  • T2: Rush Valenty Ultimate
  • T3: You either use 2 Jillian Pierce or Escanor Chop (to break the shield), then Arthur Buff and finish with Valenty ult

Phase 2

  • The main goal here is to strip his defense buff
  • The way you do this, break the shield completely. Then use a silver ignite to remove the defense buff
  • It's important to break the shield completely otherwise it will not remove the buff
  • After, you wanna stack as many ignites as possible and ulti with Escanor to kill
  • Break the shield (Escanor chop or Jillian Pierce x2) then Ultimate with Escanor
