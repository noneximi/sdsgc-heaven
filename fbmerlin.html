Boss Info

Phase 1

    Merlin
  • Passives
    • increase skill usage by 2
    • immune to all debuffs
    • for every ally dead her def decreases by 40%
    • if the enemies ultimate gauge stack is under 5, merlin gains 10% attack
    • deals 300% more damage to blue units (weakness to green heroes)
    • deals 300% more damage to Demon heroes
    • decreased damage from counter attacks by 80%
  • Skills
    • See Green Merlin
    • every 2 turns at the end, she uses perfect cube
    • Fodder
  • Passives
    • deals 300% more damage to blue units (weakness to green heroes)
    • deals 300% more damage to Demon heroes
    • immune to all debuffs
  • Skills
    • [Add 1] every first turn she casts s1 :removes buffs and inflicts poison. Single target.
    • [Add 1] every 2 turns she casts darkness s2: reduce attack skills by 20% and only allows the enemy to use attack and stance skills. Single target.
    • [Add 2] s2 deals 200% weakness damage to all enemies. weakness deals 3x more damage to all enemies who are debuffed

Phase 2

    Merlin
  • Passives - same as Phase 1 except:
    • Merlins hp is reduced by 40% from the number of allies killed
    • also wont have attack buff on under 5 gauge stack
  • Skills
    • See Red Merlin
    • Fodder
  • Passives
    • deals 300% more damage to blue units (weakness to green heroes)
    • deals 300% more damage to Demon heroes
    • immune to all debuffs
  • Skills
    • [Add 1] casts s1 on first turn: deals 450% damage to a single target enemy, removes buffs and reduces enemy ultimate gauge by 3
    • [Add 1] casts s2 every second turn: increase attack by 20% to all allies
    • [Add 2] s1 increases penetration to all allies
