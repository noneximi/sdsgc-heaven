Team Comps

The Best Comp

  • All demon raids follow a similar format. Gowther + DPS + Buffer + Sub Passive booster
  • We use Blue Slater as the main source of damage because he'll be used for Hell mode later on. He's currently the best DPS for red demon because his silver s2 can remove stances and he packs quite a punch.
  • We use Jericho over Simon as a link for our DPS units because she provides the highest attack for a link unit.

Standard F2P Comp

  • The only change to this format as above is we replace a buffer for CC. Gowther + DPS + CC + Sub Passive booster
  • We use Red Slater because he not only reduces the boss crit resistance to 0 but he also has the perfect CC chain to stop the boss from attacking. His CC removes all action except for attack and his ult is an attack seal.
  • Combined with Gowther attack seal and you can keep the boss on a permanent stand still.
  • Jericho is a great F2P DPS that shines with Red Slater and will be used for almost every single piece of content in this game.

Another F2P Comp

  • This format follows a different approach but you'll notice it is very similar. Gowther + DPS + Defensive Unit + Sub Passive booster
  • Red Elihawk is a great F2P unit to use because her damage will stack overtime. Her passive means that as you continue to fight the boss, your damage will eventually scale and hit like a truck.
  • We recommend using any CC alternative if you don't have Green Melin but she's here cause her shield makes it easier for Elihawk to stack her passive without worrying about getting hit. Green Merlin also has an ult gauge remover which helps with dealing with the boss desperation ultimate which can wipe most weaker teams.
