Ifrit Raid Guide

Guide by Arfz

Arfz#0943

Standard Whale Comp

  • Gowther + DPS + Buffer + Sub Passive booster
  • Diane and Rimuru are tied in effectiveness for Ifrit in terms of DPS. Both pack a punch due to their Rupture ability which is very effective against this boss.

Alternative Teams

  • Ifrit is quite a tough boss and so there isn't a lot of options outside of the standard whale comp for consistent extreme runs. At the end of the day other alternatives are either too weak to break down his shield or die too quickly due to his high burst.
  • Passive Sub Units - Cain, Golgius
  • Rimuru/Blue Matrona Diane - Red SR Jericho has the rupture ability. Blue Elihawk and Galand are both Amplification units that if stacked can do a resonable amount of damage but all alternative options are much weaker.
  • Buffers - Helbram, Gilthunder, or Arthur
