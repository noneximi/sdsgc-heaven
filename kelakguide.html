Kelak Normal

Guide by Arakito Ephemeral#2538

Note that this is not a max score guide, but it's made to lessen your pain and score decently without spending hours of time

Team Comp

Strategy

  • You need to reduce the damage as much as possible on your supporting units
  • Second turn get a rank up/ freeze or jeri atk card, if you don't: restart
  • Avoid using debuffs like poison if possible; you can cleanse debuffs if you have a leftover freeze, use it, then a normal attack to remove debuff
  • Note that helbram debuff should patience first turn and second turn, meaning it won't debuff the boss with poison
  • 1st turn: Clear cards, but reduce your own damage, Helbram will patience here, so is safe to use, don't debuff before you use Jeri weak point skill if you use it here
  • 2nd turn and onward, stack Jeri passive, try to avoid using weak point card if boss is debuffed
  • Stall for 5 turns, Jericho should have 5 or more stacks, if you get 3 rank up cards, you can get everything to gold, that's all within average in 5 turns, if not lvl 2 rank up will suffice with lvl 2 freeze

Average Time Spent: 15-30 mins
Average Score: 1.2k+

Kelak Hard

Team Comp

Strategy

  • This difficutly is the easiest, if you don't min max.
  • ATK Food
  • Measure your own damage and slowly build Jericho stacks
  • Shield uptime is really important, it'll get stronger the more Gow stacks you have, and Gow should be able to patience the aoe and tank ulti with shield up
  • Shield card second turn or re do
  • 1st turn: Jericho weak point skill then debuff, move rank up card to the right most (we don't want it to merge) use merlin shield
  • 2nd turn: you can either build Gowther ultimate (perfect if min maxing strategy with helb) or stack some more cards, further on just control your damage and use shield when it breaks or if is needed
  • You'll need to get 1 extra shield so it doesn't go away
  • Average RNG: 5+ Jeri stacks, lvl 2 freeze, lvl 2 rank up, 3+ Gow stacks
  • Good RNG: 6+ Jeri stacks, 3 rank up cards, shield uptime for 5 Gow stacks

Average Time Spent: 10-20 mins
Average Score: 1.2k+

Kelak Extreme

Team Comp

Strategy

  • Very similar to Normal and Hard mode
  • Rush damage for phase one and use all jericho cards you can
  • ATK Food / Crit Chance Food / Resistance Food if you're taking too much damage and not patienting the AOE
  • You'll have enough time to have the better setup
  • Average RNG: 5 or more Jericho stacks, lvl 2 freeze, the intial rank up, one shield and 3+ Gow stacks
  • Good RNG: 3 rank up card, 6+ Jeri stacks, enough shield uptime for 5 Gow stacks
  • 2nd Phase just stall if needed or buff, freeze and kill

Average Time Spent: 15-30 mins
Average Score: 1.4k+
