Team Comps

The Best Comp

  • Gowther + DPS + Buffer + Sub Passive booster
  • Green King will destory Grey with his basic gold cards. We use Jeri as a link because she's the highest attack link.

Standard F2P Comp

  • Gowther + DPS + Defense Unit + Sub Passive booster
  • Little known fact about Jericho is that although her melee attacks will miss, her combined ultimate will hit the boss.
  • As long as you stack her passive by spamming your attack cards, you'll be able to 1 shot a phase with her ultimate hitting a crit.
  • Green Merlin is here because she provides support with her shield and passively adds ultimate gauge as the fight rages on.

The Strategy

  • Phase 1
    • Stall (Use 2 trash cards and/or swap Green King cards to build ult gauge) and hope for a Gowther Silver Rank Up Card.
    • After you tank his first phase, either force a rank up on the buff or utilize the Gowther Silver + bronze to get all golds and kill with GKing.
    • Save at least 1 gold GKing attack card for Phase 2.
  • Phase 2
    • Should have GKing ult gauge full or close to it, and be able to oneshot with saved gold card(s).

Alternative Units

  • Cain - Passive Sub Unit
    • Golgius the rare unit
  • Green Merlin - Defense Unit
    • Green Elizabeth for heals and Ult gauge ultimate. Keep in mind that if Grey does give you the recovery debuff, you'll need a silver heal for purify.
  • Green King - DPS Unit
    • Green Meliodas can deal consistent damage but he's on the weaker end for damage in Grey.
    • Green Merlin can do excellent damage in Grey.
  • Helbram - Buffer
    • Gilthunder or Arthur
