Farming

  • For Fast Fort Solgres farming, run Howzer, BGuila, & RGowther, sub Green Merlin on red evolution books. Guila gets an ulti orb if she isn't damaged, and Gowther gives the team an atk buff every turn he's not damaged for a limit of 5 turns. Clearing each wave in one turn activates all these uniques. This team has really good synergy and the fastest clear times depending on your own setup and gear.
  • Howzer can solo most content if he's properly invested in. Use him solo to farm easy stages. He has AOE-only Skill Set.
  • Choose from these to build your farm team: GLizhawk, Weindhart, BGuila, Gowther and Twiggo. Check their skills, passives and synergy with each other, then decide. Howzer should always be on your farm team if possible. For sub slot you either want to have a unit to level up passively or units like red jericho and green merlin for fast clears.

Where to Farm Gold

  • The fastest and more reliable way to get a lot of money is auto farming books 24/7 on Fort Solgres 50% OFF Stamina
  • RAIDS! If you only do extreme raids, and have fast clearing times, on non stamina off days you can focus on spamming some. As drops you can get a bunch of SSR Chests, so if you can, complete a few chapters' boss battles each day so you can get chalices, gold, SSR chests, awakening mats/stones, & SR gear. To maximize your gold gains, always eat gold food in boss battles and raids.
  • Fort Solgres event gold dungeons, Only do them after you leveled all your units for box CP, otherwise keep farming on enhance dungeon with exp food and the to-level-units
  • Fort Solgres normal gold dungeon, always use these tickets, you don't lose anything and you can hold up to 10 tickets at once
  • Hawk's sales, level the 4k foods to lvl 5, and fill your 5 spots with them

What Food to Farm and/or Buy

  • PVP Coin Food
  • Gold Food
  • Hawk's Sales food till lvl 5
  • Affinity food, for your main team
  • Buy "Toast of Victory" when you want to climb faster or take full advantage of the ranked system on geared (maximum of 100 battles per week), if you lose there's no consolation price FYI
  • Buy "Toast of Defeat" when you need to do the daily for ungeared but you're ranked Champion 1 already, thus not wanting to risk a lost
  • PVP Food for a safe win (only for climbing, ranked top 100 or inefficient whale fun) like Evasion, Ulti+1, ATK (Even better with blacksmith title) and HP food are popular, decide which one is best for your team

Where to Farm Anvils & Awakening Stones

  • 1) Salvaging your gear nets you a really neat amount of awakening stones. +1 gives more red stones, +5 gives more blue stones, so:
    • 1.1) On non salvaging days, +1 your gear when on need of red stones/awakening stones, if you're not on need of any, it's wiser to save the gear and equip your base units with it, with the main goal of increasing your box cc, waiting for a salvaging event
    • 1.2) On salvaging days, +5 all the gear you've stacked that have main stats lower than 90%
  • 2) Raids, like many other stuff you can get 5* or 4* awakening stones an no stamina needed if sniping
  • 3) Guild boss for 100 weekly anvils and awakening stones
  • 4) PVP challenger: 100 to 150 anvils per week, depending if you use the rest on weapons cosmetics or not
  • 5) PVP coin farming: 100 to 150 anvils per week, depending on how much you are willing to farm with coin food, remember to reset the shop you need to use 1 diamond (which is cheap), but never do it unless you can get anvils on multiples of 100 per week
  • 6) Weapon gacha for anvils/hammer, awakening stones and gear, do this after farming on Fort day or wait on hopes of some gear gacha event
  • 7) Training cave as a source of for hammers/anvils/engraving stones. If you're unsure about using hammers, wait for 100% success event. Be wise on how you use your engraving stones, it's a limited resource bound to money or time
  • If you're constant you should be getting around 450 anvils per week and a good amount of limited resources

What Specialized Gear Sets to Build

  • 1) Specialized gear for Valenty and/or Gowther with resistance sub stats on a DEF neklace and earring So you have the possibility to change from HP/DEF, ATK/DEF or DEF/DEF/DEF with ease and with just one set
  • 2) Specialized gear for Lilia DEF bracer and ring with pierce sub stats this so you can change to ATK/DEF or HP/DEF with ease
  • Note: You can also use her with ATK substats on different teams, depends on what works with you better, and not that an ATK build lilia would have higher CC as well
  • Note: Normally using a DEF bracer and ring or DEF belt and rune allows you to have many different gear options while conserving your high rolls and exchanging between gear sets between units

Niche Builds

  • 1) CDMG set, ring and bracer Ideally, either ATK or CDMG sub stats, but be sure that these stats are over 80%. Optionally you can get the whole set Belt, Rune, Necklace and Earring
    • Note: Not the best CC, not sturdy and easy to kill, not a reliable set to use, besides some niche situations, like BDMeli max damage with a Merlin link and Death Pierce or Jericho critical damage 1st guild boss strategy, etc.
  • 2) DEF/DEF/DEF set, mainly for patience strategy, same as above you can go with either defense or resistance sub stats, when you're done with that, you can worry about min maxing
    • Note: Be aware that there's a noticeable decrease on CC by using this set and is only ideal on certain units and situations, like patience with Gowther at the cost of his own damage, adding ATK stacks over time to the party
